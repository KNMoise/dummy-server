What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lives with you by:

Advocacy

We strive to influence policies and societal norms to prevent gender-based violence and ensure justice for survivors.

Support Services

Our Platform provides critical resources, including counseling, legal assistance, and emergency shelters, to help survivors rebuild their lives.

Education and Outreach

Online workshops, campaigns, and community programs to educate the public about the impacts of gender-based violence and how to combat it.

Empowerment Programs

Through skill-building, leadership training, and empowerment initiatives, we help survivors regain their confidence and independence.

Awareness and Education

We raise awareness about GBV through campaigns, workshops, and educational programs, empowering individuals with knowledge and resources to recognize and prevent violence.

Community Engagement

We engage with communities to foster a culture of respect, equality, and zero tolerance towards GBV, encouraging active participation in creating a violence-free environment.
