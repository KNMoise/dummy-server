Let’s make a difference in the lives of others

Gender-based violence (GBV) is a pervasive and devastating issue that affects millions of individuals worldwide, regardless of age, race, or socioeconomic status. It encompasses a range of harmful behaviors directed at individuals based on their gender, including physical, sexual, emotional, and psychological abuse. GBV is a violation of fundamental human rights and has far-reaching consequences on victims, their families, and communities. The time to act is now, and we need your help to make a difference.
